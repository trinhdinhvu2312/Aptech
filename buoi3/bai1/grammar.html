nouns

A noun represents a person, place, animal, thing or an idea.

kind of nouns

Nouns are classified as follows:

  • Proper:It is the name of a particular object.
  • Common:It is the name possessed bt things in general.
  • Collective:It is the name representing a group of things.
  • Abstract:It is the name representing quantities.

Pronouns

A pronoun is a word used in place of a noun.

kind of pronouns

Pronouns are classified as follows:

  • Personal pronouns:It refers to a specific noun.
  • Interrogative:It stands as the answer to the question about a person, place or a thing.
  • Indefinite pronouns:It is used to refer to a person, thing or a place that are unknown.
  • Relavetive pronouns:It is the name representing quantities.
  • Bemonstrative pronouns:It is the name representing quantities.
  • Reflexive pronouns:It is the name representing quantities.
